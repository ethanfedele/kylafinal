Zach Cato
Secretary

Zach Cato is an associate attorney at Kriz, Jenkins, Prewitt & Jones, PSC. His practice is devoted to civil defense litigation. Previously, Zach served as Senior Conflicts Analyst for Morgan Lewis & Bockius (formerly Bingham McCutchen LLP). He received his Bachelor of Science in Business Administration and Marketing from the University of Evansville ('08), a Master of Business Administration from the University of Kentucky Gatton College of Business and Economics ('10), and a Juris Doctor from the University of Kentucky College of Law ('13). During law school Zach served as an Articles Editor for the Kentucky Law Journal and as a research assistant to Professor Brian Frye. Zach has worked with Kentucky Lawyers for the Arts since its conception in 2012.
